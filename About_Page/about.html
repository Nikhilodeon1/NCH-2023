The climate crisis is an existential threat to coral. The algae in the coral get expelled when the coral is under stress. This is called Bleaching and leaves the coral gray and white.

While coral can recover from bleaching, it's still very bad. The algae in the coral, called zooxanthellae, are the coral's main source of food and color. Without them, the coral has a very high chance of starving.
